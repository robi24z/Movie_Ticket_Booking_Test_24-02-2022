Our can book tickets only through TicketNew website and app. We do not process phone/email requests from users. Please note that TicketNew or its employees will never contact you requesting for your bank account number/ card number/ card expiry date/ CVV/ OTP/ net banking password to help you book movie tickets. Do not share these details with anyone over phone or e-mail as this could lead to fraudulent transactions.

Are you a new user? No worries! All you have to do is, Click ‘Register’ option on the homepage, and fill up Name, Email ID, Contact and other required details. After submitting it, you will be receiving a confirmation message on-screen, along with a mail on your registered email ID. Now, you can just log in using the given email ID, password or mobile number, and book movie tickets in a hassle-free way.

The customers should enter the given login id (email id that you registered in bookmyticket.com), and then choose their preferred location, theatres, show timings, no. of seats. The site then redirects the user to the online transaction page where they will be given a choice to make payment availing offers through wallets, credit or debit cards.

Children aged 3 yrs and above require a ticket to enter the cinema hall.
